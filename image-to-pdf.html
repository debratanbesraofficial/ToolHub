How to Convert Images to PDF in 4 Easy Steps

1. Select Your Images

Click the upload area to choose image files from your computer, or simply drag and drop your images into the dashed box.

2. Arrange and Set Options

Your images will appear as previews. Set your desired PDF page size (e.g., A4) and orientation (Portrait/Landscape).

3. Generate PDF

Once your images are uploaded and options are set, click the "Generate PDF" button to start the conversion process.

4. Download PDF

After generation is complete, a download button will appear. Click it to save your newly created PDF file.
