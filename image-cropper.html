How to Crop Images in 4 Easy Steps

1. Upload Your Image

Click the upload area to choose an image from your computer, or simply drag and drop it here.

2. Adjust Crop Area

Drag the handles on the image to select your desired crop area. Choose a predefined aspect ratio or freehand crop.

3. Crop Image

Once you are happy with the selection, click the "Crop Image" button to apply the crop.

4. Download Result

After cropping is complete, a download button will appear. Click it to save your new cropped image.
