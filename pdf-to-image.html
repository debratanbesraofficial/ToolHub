How to Convert PDF to Image in 4 Easy Steps

1. Select Your PDF

Click on the upload area to choose a PDF file from your computer, or simply drag and drop your file into the dashed box.

2. Choose Your Options

Select your desired image format (JPG or PNG) and resolution. You can also specify a page range (e.g., "1-3, 5") to convert only specific pages.

3. Start Conversion

Once your file is uploaded and options are set, click the "Convert" button to begin the process. Wait for the progress bar to reach 100%.

4. Download Images

After conversion, your images will appear below. Download them individually or click the "ZIP" button to get all images in a single archive file.
